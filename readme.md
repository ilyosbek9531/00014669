“Asaxiy” project, which is considered a big project in the development of online trade in Uzbekistan, has been helping many people. Users can find all the products they want quickly and easily on this site, and delivery service is also available. I tried to prepare a version of this site using html, css, js technologies. Although the site I have prepared is not as rich in functions as the real one, but basically all the possibilities have been created for the user to find the product he wants in a quick, easy and convenient way. You can buy 99 types of goods through the website. You have to go through several steps to find the product you like. First of all, you can sort the products according to the country of manufacture, and also the products are paginated, and when the user selects the desired number, 10 products will be displayed and without breaking the rest of the steps, you can sort the products by alphabetical order, by page order, and by the year of creation. Most importantly, you can search by product name and see the result when you click the search button and you can also add products to the shopping cart. In the footer section of the website, you can get acquainted with other information about “Asaxiy”.
